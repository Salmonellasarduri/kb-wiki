# Experiments Ring the ‘Death Knell’ for Sterile Neutrinos

Decades of anomalous experimental results (neutrino oscillation anomalies) had suggested the existence of sterile neutrinos, hypothetical particles that would explain multiple physics mysteries. Recent experiments have effectively ruled them out, leaving the original anomalies unexplained.
