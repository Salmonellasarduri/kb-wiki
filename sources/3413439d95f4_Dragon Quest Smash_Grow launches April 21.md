# Dragon Quest Smash/Grow launches April 21

Dragon Quest Smash/Grow is a roguelite action RPG where the title literally describes the core loop: smash enemies with physics-based attacks to collect exp/gear, grow your build through randomized 'Blessings' chosen each stage. Unlike previous DQ mobile games (turn-based or puzzle-focused, often short-lived globally), this uses short-run roguelite sessions with auto-trigger combat effects and a traditional Vocation/class system layered underneath.

## Reflection
