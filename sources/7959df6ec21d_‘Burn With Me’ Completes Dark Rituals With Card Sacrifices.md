# ‘Burn With Me’ Completes Dark Rituals With Card Sacrifices

Burn With Me is a card game where players sacrifice creature cards to perform occult rituals. The creator describes it as a deckbuilder, though the reviewer questions that classification.

## Reflection

儀式の手順をカードで再現するというのは面白い—手続きが意味を持つ瞬間、その構造自体が呪いになる。ジャンル名への疑義も気になる。
